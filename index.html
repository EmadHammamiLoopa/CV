uAMBIT: Byte-Budgeted Federated Compression

Independent Research / University of Oslo

Federated learning compression method enforcing a strict 0.5 KiB/client/round uplink budget with competitive accuracy vs. Top-k, STC, QSGD, and EF-Sign baselines. Accepted at IEEE DCOSS-IoT 2026.

  • Threshold-based sparse compression with scale sharing & error-feedback
  • Benchmarked on BreastMNIST with TensorFlow; reproducible Colab experiments
  • Full single-notebook experiment + modular src/ package
